How to use your dashboard

US Stock Expert Advisor — a quick guide.

DashboardMy PortfolioGuide

1. The two pages

Dashboard shows buy/sell signals for the stocks you choose. My Portfolio tracks what you own (stocks & options) with profit/loss and a suggested action. Switch between them with the links at the top. Bookmark port.080342.xyz on your phone. If something looks old, refresh the page.

2. Pick your watchlist (do this first)

On the Dashboard, click ⚙ Manage watchlist. You'll see ~95 stocks grouped by sector with a search box.

  • Select all / Deselect all — choose or clear everything at once.
  • each sector has a "select all / none" toggle — grab a whole sector in one click.
  • or tick individual stocks. Use search to find a ticker fast.
  • Click Save watchlist when done.

To use the same watchlist on phone and PC, type your username + PIN (same login as the portfolio) in the panel and Log in before saving. Without login it saves on that device only.

3. Read a stock card

Price + % change at the top (green = up, red = down). A small green ● dot means the price is live.

The big coloured badge is the call: BUY ACCUMULATE HOLD TRIM SELL

The bars (RSI, Trend, MACD, News) show why: filled to the right/green = bullish, to the left/red = bearish.

The score gauge (−100 to +100) needle: far right = strong buy, far left = strong sell, middle = neutral. Tap the news links at the bottom to read headlines.

Golden rule: a signal is strongest when the bars agree (trend + momentum + RSI all leaning the same way). One bar alone is weak.

4. The controls

  • Signal pills (e.g. "3 BUY, 5 HOLD") — a tally of your watchlist.
  • Sort — by sector, or strongest buy / strongest sell first.
  • Filter — show only Buy/Accumulate or only Sell/Trim.
  • Search — jump to one ticker.
  • Gauges row (SPY/QQQ/DIA/IWM) — overall market mood.

5. Live prices vs. closed

During US market hours you'll see ● LIVE · prices updated [time] and prices refresh every 30s. Outside hours it shows the last close. The buy/sell signals always use the end-of-day close — only the price and % go live.

6. Extras

USD ↔ THB converter (type an amount, flip the direction) and the 🌙/☀️ dark-mode toggle, both near the top.

7. A simple daily routine

  • Glance at the gauges and signal pills for the big picture.
  • Sort by "strongest buy first" to see what's lining up.
  • Check the candidate's bars agree, then tap its news links.
  • Decide your move; record it in My Portfolio to track P/L and get add / hold / take-profit guidance.

Not financial advice. This is educational decision-support — signals don't predict the future or guarantee returns; markets carry risk of loss. Size sensibly, use a stop, and you place every trade yourself.
