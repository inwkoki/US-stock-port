How the suggested action is decided
It combines two things — your profit/loss and the advisor signal (RSI + trend + momentum + news):
  • SELL signal → take profit if up, cut if down (signal turned bearish).
  • TRIM signal → lock in some profit if up ≥20%; review if near your stop.
  • BUY / ACCUMULATE → add (especially near or below your cost); let winners run, add on dips.
  • HOLD → hold; consider trimming a large gain (≥30%); review if down ≥8% (near stop-loss).
A suggested stop-loss flag appears when a position is down ≥8% from your cost.

Not financial advice. Educational decision-support only — you place every trade yourself. Suggestions blend rule-based signals with your P/L; they don't predict the future. Prices are a snapshot unless you update them. Data is stored only in this browser/device.
